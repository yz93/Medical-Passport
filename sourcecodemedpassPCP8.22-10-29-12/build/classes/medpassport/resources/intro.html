This is a mobile Medical Passport that helps patients carry their own health records at all times. Only Physicians are allowed to add or delete information in the Medical Passport. Any information that looks suspicious to the reader should be reported to the Primary Care Physician. The Primary Care Physicians name and phone number can be found on the page related to the patient’s personal information.
